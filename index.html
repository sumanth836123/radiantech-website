Our Capabilities

What We Do

Mission-critical RF intelligence built on FPGA silicon, integrating wideband sensing with AI-driven classification and adaptive beam control.

Radar Detection
Continuous wide-area spectrum scanning that detects airborne threats the moment they appear — day or night, in complex electromagnetic environments.
Direction Finding
Pinpoints the exact bearing of a hostile drone using smart beam-steering antenna arrays — no multiple installations required, no guesswork.
Signal Classification
AI instantly identifies the make, model, and operator intent of a detected drone — distinguishing hobbyist craft from malicious threats in real time.
Soft-Kill Jamming
Surgically disrupts drone control links on confirmed threats, forcing them to land or return — without affecting surrounding communications infrastructure.
Operator Dashboard
A clear, intuitive command interface gives operators instant situational awareness — live spectrum view, threat timeline, and one-click response actions.
Edge Processing
All intelligence runs on-device with no cloud dependency — ensuring zero latency, full operational security, and mission-readiness in disconnected environments.

About Us

Radiantech Systems

Radiantech Systems Pvt. Ltd. is an Indian deep-tech defence company building the next generation of counter-drone intelligence systems. We engineer compact, deployable platforms that give security teams complete airspace awareness — without requiring specialist knowledge to operate.

Our team combines deep backgrounds in defence electronics, artificial intelligence, and systems engineering — converging these disciplines into products that address the rapidly growing threat posed by commercial and hostile unmanned aerial systems.

We are actively developing our flagship RADIAN-S1 platform and engaging with defence establishments, critical infrastructure operators, and strategic investors for pilot deployments and long-term partnerships.
